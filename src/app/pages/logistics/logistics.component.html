Haulage /Distribution

Our clients are the centre of everything that we do. Our approach is to harness our people, assets and robust IT Platform to create achievable goals and deliver sustainable results.

In line with our expansion strategy, Trig Energy offers reliable, timely and safe logistics services in the downstream sub-sector of the oil and gas industry.

The company primarily focuses on transporting petroleum products (JET A1) from oil depots to the desired destination across the country, our trucks have sophisticated tracking systems which enables us gain information of their location at any particular point in time.

We have drivers who are helpful, courteous and fully trained to handle hazardous materials. Our trucks are well-maintained and inspected regularly for safety and comfort. We promote the life span of our truck by ensuring and putting in place systematic maintenance routines.

We ensure that there is no leakage of fuel and deliver the exact volume of petroleum products to our customers.

We offer competitive rates to our customers because we have low cost inputs.
